Sausage Pasta Fazool

Description

Who doesn't like pasta already? What makes this recipe even better is that is it extra saucy.

If you're tired of waiting to go to an Italian restaurant to taste your favorite pasta, then you're in luck.

Ingredients

  • three hot italian sausages
  • chicken stock
  • seasoning
  • macaroni
  • tomato paste

Steps

  1. uncase your sausages
  2. heat up your pot to medium-high heat, then add oil
  3. sautee your sausages until golden brown
  4. add macaroni (raw)
  5. mix well, and add tomato paste
  6. add chicken stock and let simmer until macaroni is cooked
  7. VOILA, you have your pasta.
