Pancakes

Description

These are protein rich pancakes that only require four ingredients.

If you're looking for macro-friendly yummy pancakes that are also quick to make, then these are just right for you.

Ingredients

  • Pancake Mix
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Almond Milk
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a bowl
  2. Preheat pan to medium heat
  3. Add cooking spray
  4. Add a spoonful of the mix
  5. Cook one side until you see bubbles, and then flip
